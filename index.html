The Craft

What is Decanting?

Decanting is the art of transferring perfume from its original bottle into a smaller vial. This lets you explore a wide variety of high-end fragrances affordably, test scents on your skin before committing to a full bottle, and carry your favorite aromas anywhere. At Sole Whiff, we use precise, sterile techniques to ensure every decant is a perfect, authentic sample of the original masterpiece.
